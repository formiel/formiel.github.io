Welcome to my blog!

I am currently a PhD Candidate in Speech and Language Processing at the GETALP team of Laboratoire d’Informatique de Grenoble (LIG), a joint research team between Université Grenoble Alpes and CNRS. Prior to that, I obtained my MSc in Data Sciences and Business Analytics at CentraleSupélec & ESSEC. I work on multilingual and low-resource speech translation.
